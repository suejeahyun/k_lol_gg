K-LOL.GG Auction Card Foley Sound Patch

The WAV files in this folder are procedurally generated for this patch.
They do not include external game audio, commercial sample packs, or third-party recordings.

Design direction:
- card fan spread: light playing-card flourish
- player select: one card drawn from a spread
- confirm: soft card placement
- tier step: minimal game UI shimmer
- diamond/master tension: non-percussive airy riser/pad
- card flip: paper/card flip, no sci-fi whoosh
- reveal: soft game-like shimmer, no drum impact

Usage: safe to use inside the K-LOL.GG project.
